About Our Division


UCSD Circle K is a part of Paradise Division, one of the eight divisions in Cal-Nev-Ha (see District tab). Our current Lieutenant Governor is San Diego State University's Amanda Spendlove. Our division comprises seven schools that range in location from California to Hawaii. Paradise Division's mascot is a toucan.
